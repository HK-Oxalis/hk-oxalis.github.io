A simple driving game about exploring a peaceful environment made for the Cozy Fall Jam 2024. Made solo in Godot, making heavy use of Blender's Geometry nodes and with stock music.


Retrospective thoughts

As my first foray into Geometry Nodes, I'm happy with what I managed. As a game that I wanted other people to play, I'm not thrilled with the performance, and I forgot that Godot 4 didn't support C# on web, so I couldn't release a web build. It certainly captures the right 'vibe', however, and it was good to get my head around Godot's 3D side.
